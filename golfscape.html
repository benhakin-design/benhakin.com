golfscape

The future of golf technology. The vision? To support the development of the industry by building a modern infrastructure for golfers and courses to connect.

golfscape is the industry leader providing its reservations and operations services in over 30 golf destinations across Europe, Africa, Near East, and Asia-Pacific. golfscape's OS platform delivers course operators next-generation tee sheets, member and visitor mobile apps, as well as revenue-generating technologies and services.

As an in-house web designer at golfscape, my main focus is on the design & development of the front-end & UI for golfscape's marketplace, mobile app, and online products. Beginning with creating a solid idea and an understanding of the goal at hand through high-quality mockups and prototypes, which then I'll develop using technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, SASS, and Javascript.
